INDIA

India, officially the Republic of India(ISO:Bharat Ganarajya), is a country in South Asia. it is the senenth-largets country by area; the most populous country as of June 2023; and from the time of its independence in 1947, the world's populous democracy. Bounded by the Indian Ocean on the south, The Arabian Sea on the Southwest and the Bay of Bengal on the southeast, it shares land borders with pakistan to the west;China,Nepal and Bhutan to the north and Bangladesh and Mynamar to the east. In the Indian Ocean, India is in the vicinity of Sri Lanka and the Maldives; its Andaman and Nicobar Islands share a maritime border with Thailand, Mynmar, and Indonesia.
