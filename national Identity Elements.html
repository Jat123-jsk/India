National Flag

The National Flag is a horizontal tricolour of India saffron at the top, white in the middle and India green at the bottom in equal proportion

National Anthem

The song Jana-gana-mana, composed originally in Bangla by Rabindranath Tagore, was adopted in its Hindi version by the Constituent Assembly as the National Anthem of India on January 24,1950.

National Song

The song Vande Mataram composed in sanskrit by Bankimchandra chatterji, was a source of inspi freedom. It has an equal status with Jana-gana-mana.

National Bird

The Indian peacock, pavo cristatus, the national bird of India, is a colourful, swan-sized bird with a fan under the eye and a long slender neck.
